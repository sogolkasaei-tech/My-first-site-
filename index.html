Wuthering Heights by Emily Brontë (Les Hauts de Hurlevent)

Wuthering Heights is a classic novel written by Emily Brontë. It tells the intense and tragic love story between Heathcliff and Catherine Earnshaw. The novel explores themes of love, revenge, and social class in the wild moors of England.

One important part of the story shows the destructive power of passion and obsession. You can read more about the characters on this study guide .
